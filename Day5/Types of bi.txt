
📊 Understanding Types of Filters in Power BI

Filters are essential in Power BI because they help users focus on specific data and gain meaningful insights.

Power BI provides three main types of filters:

🔹 Visual-Level Filters
Applied to a single visual and affect only that chart or table.

🔹 Page-Level Filters
Applied to all visuals on a specific report page.

🔹 Report-Level Filters
Applied across the entire report and affect all pages.

💡 Quick Tip:
Visual-Level → One visual
Page-Level → One page
Report-Level → Entire report

Understanding how these filters work helps create more interactive and user-friendly dashboards.
