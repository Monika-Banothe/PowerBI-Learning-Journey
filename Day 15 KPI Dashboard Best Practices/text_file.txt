📊 KPI Dashboard Best Practices | Power BI

A dashboard is more than just charts and numbers—it's a decision-making tool.

A well-designed KPI dashboard helps stakeholders quickly understand business performance and focus on what matters most.

Here are a few best practices I always keep in mind:

✅ Define clear business objectives
✅ Focus on the most important KPIs
✅ Keep the design simple and uncluttered
✅ Choose the right visuals for the data
✅ Group related metrics logically
✅ Add filters and slicers for better exploration
✅ Maintain consistent colors and formatting
✅ Design for readability across devices
✅ Continuously review and improve the dashboard

💡 Key Takeaway:

A great KPI dashboard doesn't show more data—it shows the right data in the right way.
