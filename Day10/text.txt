📊 5 Common Mistakes in Power BI That Can Impact Your Reports

Building a dashboard is easy.

Building an efficient, scalable, and insightful dashboard is where the real challenge begins.

Some common mistakes I often see (and have learned to avoid):

❌ Poor data modeling
❌ Too many visuals on a page
❌ Overly complex DAX formulas
❌ Ignoring report performance
❌ Skipping data validation

💡 A successful Power BI report isn't just about attractive visuals.

It's about:
✔ Clean data
✔ Strong data models
✔ Optimized performance
✔ Clear business insights

Small improvements in these areas can make a huge difference in report quality and user experience.
