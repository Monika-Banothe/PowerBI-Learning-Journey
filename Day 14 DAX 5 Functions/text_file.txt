📊 Top 5 DAX Functions Every Beginner Should Learn | Power BI

DAX is the heart of Power BI. Understanding the right functions can help you create dynamic measures, solve business problems, and build impactful dashboards.

In this carousel, I've covered 5 essential DAX functions that every beginner should know:

✅ CALCULATE() – Modify filter context and create powerful calculations.
✅ SUMX() – Perform row-by-row calculations before aggregation.
✅ FILTER() – Return rows based on custom conditions.
✅ ALL() – Remove filters for comparisons and percentage calculations.
✅ SUM() – Aggregate values from a single column.
