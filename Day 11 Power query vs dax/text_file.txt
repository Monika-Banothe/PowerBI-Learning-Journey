📊 **Power Query vs DAX – Know the Difference**

Many beginners think Power Query and DAX do the same job—but they don't.

🔹 **Power Query** prepares and transforms data before it is loaded into the model.

🔹 **DAX** performs calculations and creates business logic after the data is loaded.

💡 Simple way to remember:

**Power Query = Prepare your data**
**DAX = Analyze your data**

Both are essential skills for building efficient and insightful Power BI reports.
